Why choose us?

Hello, my name is Moussa, I'm almost twenty years old, I live in Morocco at the moment and I have been tutoring people languages online since 2020. the way I teach languages isn't formal and it's focused mainly on speaking and listening, I don't like to be called a "Teacher", I prefer being called a "Friend" or a "Language buddy", this way students are not gonna be afraid to make mistakes because it's not a Teacher - Student relationship, it's just a Friend trynna help you out. during my class you'll learn a lot of vocab and grammar rules as well as pronunciation rules, on top of that we're gonna play a couple of fun games that are gonna get you familiar with the language and the culture!
